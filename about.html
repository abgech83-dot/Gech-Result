About the Result Analysis System

This specialized application optimizes academic grading workflows by combining multi-role architecture with automated record sorting.

Key System Implementations:

  • Dynamic Filtering: Filters grade 9 and 10 profiles directly by section. Grade 11 and 12 profiles are filtered by their streaming focus (Natural or Social Science) before section mapping.
  • Automated Credentials: Generates customized, role-based login profiles securely upon registration.
  • Excel Workbook Integrations: Enables instructors to import complex student score data or download grading sheets instantly via standard `.xlsx` files.
  • Comprehensive Data Analysis: Automatically breaks down performance records into clear statistical performance brackets: Below 50, 50–74, and 75–100.
